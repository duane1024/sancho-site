Operator takeaways

  • Reliability matters most when it supports shipping, not when it becomes the whole story.
  • Thin triggers and owned API workflows age better than business logic spread across edge hooks.
  • Growth systems are real product work: capture, follow-up, segmentation, and feedback loops all count.
  • Creative tooling gets more useful when presets, logs, and local setup make it repeatable.
  • Tests belong on the handoff boundaries, not just the sunny-day path.
  • Working systems should leave tracks that another operator can follow.
